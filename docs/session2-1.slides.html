Caveats for the Presentation of Data¶

Presentation of data should be

  • effective
  • unambiguous and precise
  • accessible

For ethical reasons, make sure to

  • show relevant facts
  • not conceal unfavorable findings

Caveats for the Presentation of Data (cont.)¶
